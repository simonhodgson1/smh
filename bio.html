Biography

Simon Hodgson lives and works on the Central Coast of New South Wales. Simon completed his BFA with Honours (sculpture) at the National Art School in 2012. On graduating he was awarded the Robin Gibson Gallery prize and his since exhibited regularly with the gallery and will be having his first solo show with RRg in june 2014.

In 2013 he has been selected as a finalist in the Gosford Art Prize and Sculpture in the Vineyards.
